# Privacy Policy for Lent Scanner

**Last Updated:** February 2026

## Overview

Lent Scanner ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
- **Email address** — Used to create and authenticate your account with Supabase
- **Account creation date** — Automatically recorded when you sign up

### Scan History
- **Product barcodes** — The barcodes you scan
- **Product names and ingredients** — Pulled from the Open Food Facts database
- **Scan results** — Whether the product is safe, not safe, or caution for Orthodox Christian fasting
- **Scan timestamps** — When you scanned each product

### Recipe Preferences
- **Favorite recipes** — Recipes you save to your favorites list
- **Recipe data** — Full recipe details including ingredients and instructions

## How We Use Your Data

- **Authentication** — Your email is used to secure your account and sync your data across devices
- **Data sync** — Scan history and favorites are stored in Supabase so you can access them from multiple devices
- **Service improvement** — We do not share your data with third parties or use it for analytics

## Data Storage

- **Scan history** — Stored securely in Supabase (encrypted in transit, row-level security enforced)
- **Favorite recipes** — Stored in Supabase; you own your data and can delete it anytime
- **Authentication tokens** — Encrypted on-device using the phone's secure storage

## Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by signing out and requesting account deletion through the Supabase console.

## Third-Party Services

- **Open Food Facts** — We query their free API for product ingredient data. See their privacy policy: https://world.openfoodfacts.org/privacy
- **Spoonacular** — We query their API for recipe data. See their privacy policy: https://spoonacular.com/food-api/privacy
- **Supabase** — We use Supabase for authentication and data storage. See their privacy policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your data anytime by logging into your account
- Delete your account and all data associated with it
- Request a copy of your data
